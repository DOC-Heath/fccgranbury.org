Amy Piatt

Minister of Discipleship

Amy Piatt is wife to Christian and mom to Mattias and Zoe. She is passionate about helping congregations build bridges from the Church that they have inherited to the Church that God is calling them to become. Amy has direct experience with new church planting, revitalization, strategic planning and community engagement in social justice. Amy received her M.Div. from Brite Divinity School at TCU. She loves singing, preaching, pastoral care and counseling, creative worship, and storytelling. She and Christian co-host a podcast, The Homebrewed Culturecast, where they discuss matters of faith, culture, and current events from a Christian perspective. Amy is a lover of people, a motivator, teacher, encourager and visionary. She enjoys helping people see what is possible and then setting them free to do good work in the world, and she also loves making up songs about any topic, dark chocolate, hiking, puppies and HGTV. Deshay Jackson

Minister Of Music

Motivated, enthusiastic scholar of music and theology, with a passion for education and interest in active engagement in student learning, DeShay is on the move and ready to make a difference in the field of music and ministry. With previous success in instruction and performance rooted in true collaboration, natural leadership, and personal dedication DeShay strives to make music an enjoyable experience for everyone.

DeShay is from New London, MO and is a fourth-generation Disciple of Christ. In December 2017, they completed a degree in Music Education from Culver-Stockton College with an emphasis in Piano and Voice. They studied voice with Dr. Elliot Jones and Ms. Christine Armistead, and piano with Dr. Dylan Marney and the late Dr. Anda Zirnitis.

During their time at Culver-Stockton College, DeShay was musically involved with the Symphonic Band, Wind Ensemble, Jazz Ensemble, Camerata 808 Ensemble, Collegiate Choir, and Concert Choir. Their off-campus activities included performances with the Quincy Concert Band, Quincy Community Theatre, Muddy River Opera Company in Quincy, IL, Union Avenue Opera Company in St. Louis, MO, Big River Swing Machine in Quincy, IL, and the Bullis-Rutter Big Band in Keokuk, IA. DeShay has served as the choir director and pianist for Canton Christian Church in Canton, MO, and Music Minister for St. Martin-in-the-Fields Episcopal Church in Keller, TX.

DeShay is a Master of Divinity student seeking certificates in Black Church Studies and Gender/Sexual Justice at Brite Divinity. They are absolutely thrilled to be serving in a DOC church, loves sloths and jazz, and cannot wait to see what these next few years have in store. Soli Deo Gloria!

Email DeShay at deshay@fccgranbury.org
