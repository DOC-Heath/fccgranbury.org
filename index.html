Our View On Stewardship

Every year (usually in October) we have a stewardship campaign.

This is a time we challenge each person in our church to assess where they are in the 5 areas of discipleship (Prayer, Study, Service, Presence & Generosity).

Each of these is an opportunity to give: of our time, our love, and our financial resources.

Part of this season is filling out an annual pledge card. If you missed submitting your pledge in October, it’s never to late. There are some physical cards in the Narthex of the church, or you can pledge online. Stewardship Pledge

Children and Youth

FCC Loves Children and Youth! Learn more about our philosophy for our children’s ministries and resources available to your family.

Infants & Toddlers

To support our parents with infants and toddlers, we have a mothers room and a staffed nursery available to 5 and under.

Children bring us so much joy! We invite them to join us in service, but if you prefer there are several options.

Staffed Nursery for 5 & Under

Our nursery is staffed with trained individuals. You can enjoy worship knowing your children are in a safe environment. The link below will allow you to pre-register your child, or you can do so on Sunday mornings. Registration only needs to be completed one time per child, but can be updated anytime. pre-register your child

Mothers’ Room

Our new Mothers’ Room is located between the kitchen and childrens’ wing. Worship services are streamed into the room, and there are recliners, nursing pillows, a changing area, and baby bouncers/swings available. This room also has some parenting resources available, as well as “library” to take home or share baby equipment.

Prayground

In our “Gathering Area” (where you’ll find coffee, donuts and more in the mornings) you’ll see a table and some shelves with toys appropriate for toddlers and young elementary-aged kids. Feel free to spend time here if your kids need a break from worship service. The service is streamed onto the TVs and there are some bibles/hymnals and games available as well!

Children

Ages 3 through 1st grade can attend the Montessori-style Worship & Wonder. 2nd through 5th grades attend JYF where they study the fundamentals of our faith.

Our pre-school and elementary students are changing so quickly, and we love to see them learn and grow. We have organized faith programming as well as plenty of time for them to have fun and be kids. We love that our children LOVE being at church and many of them consider this to be where they’ve found their best friends.

Children’s Worship & Wonder (Ages 3 to 7)

Worship & Wonder is a holistic, montessori style program that uses storytelling, routine and fellowship to deepen children’s relationship with God. The children sing, hear a story, enjoy communion/”a feast”, and create together weekly. Everything there is guided by the childrens’ own understanding and wonder.

Junior Youth Fellowship (JYF) (2nd Grade to 5th Grade)

This class lays the foundation for our older student programs, Chi-Rho and CYF. Here, students become more familiar with the bible and learn more about the fundamentals of Christian faith.

Student Ministries

Our Chi-Rho program for middle schoolers and CYF program for high schoolers are where young disciples grow through love and service.

Our students are a full part of our congregation. Below, you will see special opportunities we have for them, but they are also involved in church leadership, volunteering with younger children, and developing their own spiritual practices.

Chi-Rho

Chi-Rho is for junior high students. Quite often, they participate with the senior high students, but there are special camps and retreats for this age.

Christian Youth Fellowship (CYF)

Our senior high students participate in CYF. There are a lot of service opportunities and they learn more in depth theology during Sunday School classes and Youth Group Wednesday Nights.

OUR MISSION: to share with ALL people the unconditional love of God that we experience in Jesus Christ our Lord

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be a church that helps people COME ALIVE IN CHRIST by inviting them to reimagine church with us:

Not pride and judgment, but a sanctuary for restoring the soul – humble people sharing radical hospitality, and loving each other beyond our differences.

Not easy answers, but growing together through shared engagement with the scriptures and the hard questions of life and faith.

Not a self-serving institution, but a community in the community that truly sees our neighbor and that responds with the relational love of Christ.
